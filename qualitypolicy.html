QUALITY POLICY

At Linbuch Global Resources Limited, we are committed to the provision of procurement/Logistics
& General Services & maintenance, which consistently conform to our customer's specified
requirement and meet applicable national and International regulatory, codes and standards.
Our Quality Policy is centered around continuous improvement, customer satisfaction, and
adherence to industry standards.

We are dedicated to providing a safe and healthy work environment for our employees while
minimizing our environmental impact. Through teamwork, integrity, and accountability, we aim to be a
trusted partner in the global market. We strive to exceed our customers' expectations by maintaining a
culture of excellence, fostering innovation, and ensuring that all our processes are efficient and
effective.

To achieve this, Linbuch Global Resources Limited has established and maintains an effective and
efficient quality management system within our environment for continuous improvement which is
appropriate to the context of our organization and supports our strategic direction. We annually review
our performance, customers' satisfaction indicators and formally generate goals and targets in line with
our strategic direction and corporate objectives.

Employees at all levels in our organization shall be made aware of the company's quality policy and their
individual roles in our overall bid to achieving the main drive of this policy. The Quality Policy shall be
made available to relevant interested parties as appropriate. The Quality Policy and objectives are regularly
reviewed for continued suitability.
