OCCUPATIONAL HEALTH AND SAFETY POLICY


LINBUCH GLOBAL RESOURCES LIMITED:

  • Is committed to ensuring a safe and healthy working environment for all its employees.
  • Believes that most work-related injuries and diseases are preventable and that a “zero accident” target is desirable and achievable.
  • Recognizes that its management have primary responsibility for providing and maintaining a working environment that is safe and without risks to health.
  • Aims to prevent workplace accidents, injuries, and illnesses through proactive measures and continuous improvement.

THESE OBJECTIVES WILL BE MET BY:

  • Providing a safe system of work to prevent the ongoing effects of work-related injuries and illnesses. The commitment of senior mamagement and employees to comply with a jointly developed Occupational Health and Safety System, including plans, procedures and programs for the purpose of eliminating workplace hazards and providing a healthy and safe working environment.
  • Ensuring employees understand their responsibility to avoid risks to themselves and others and to report all unsafe and unhealthy practices and conditions.
  • Providing the necessary training and safety equipment to enable all employees to comply with the Occupational Health and Safety System including relevant national and international legislation and Codes of Practice affecting the workplace.
  • Select sub-contractors and partners whose safety standards are acceptable to ours and whose commitment to safe work practices can be clearly and continuously demonstrated.
  • The display of this policy in all confirms our commitment to maintaining a safe place of work for all employees.
